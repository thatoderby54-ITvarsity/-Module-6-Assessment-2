Welcome Auto4Sale Company

The Auto4Sale Company, is an American multinational automaker that has its main headquarters in Dearborn, Michigan, a suburb of Detroit. It was founded by Henry Ford and incorporated on June 16, 1903. The company sells automobiles and commercial vehicles under the Ford brand, and most luxury cars under the Lincoln brand. Ford also owns Brazilian SUV manufacturer Troller, an 8% stake in Aston Martin of the United Kingdom and a 32% stake in Jiangling Motors.

It also has joint-ventures in China (Changan Auto), Taiwan (Auto Lio Ho), Thailand (AutoAlliance Thailand), Turkey (Auto Otosan), and Russia (Auto Sollers). The company is listed on the New York Stock Exchange and is controlled by the Ford family; they have minority ownership but the majority of the voting power.
